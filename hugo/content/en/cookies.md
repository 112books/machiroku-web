## Cookie Policy

### 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you browse a website. They are used to remember your preferences and improve your experience.

### 2. Types of cookies we use

- **Essential cookies:** Necessary for the website to function.
- **Analytics cookies:** Allow us to analyze how users use the site to improve it.

### 3. Managing cookies

You can configure your browser to accept or reject cookies. If you reject cookies, some features of the site may not work properly.

### 4. More information

For more information about cookies, visit www.aboutcookies.org
